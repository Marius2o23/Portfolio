About Me

Hey there, I'm Marius.
If I were to go back in time when I was 10 years old and I would have been asked what I would like to be when I grow up, I would have answered "I don't know what the future looks like but if I don't have a computer I don't want to be there".
Well I dint't knew that technology would evolve so much.
No matter that 2, 5, 10 years have passed, my dream is still there and waiting to be fulfilled.
I consider that I was a different child because while my friends were playing I was fascinated by technology in the true sense of the word.
I wanted to disassemble everything I could, to discover the components and assemble them in place.
When my mother saw that I was taking apart the computer instead of playing, she said "This child either has a problem or will become a technology genius".
I haven't gotten there yet but this is my dream.
Growing up, I discovered more and more information, I was starting to wonder how a website is created and what is behind an application, until the day I said it was my time, so I started a course and really studied.
I'm 32 years old, I'm at the age where I know what I want, I want to work in the IT field and discover everything technology has to offer.
Let's make dreams come true.
I want to make that 10-year-old proud.
